2017 New york Summer Fashion

In New York, a huge fashion trend was off the shoulder tops. This was a trend that again was popular in the 80's but has come back again. Off hte shoulder tops were made in all different colors, materials, and style of shirts. There were off the shoulder tops from blouses, sweaters, short sleeves, and long sleeves. Hundreds of brands has incorporated this style into their products. This was also a fairly cheap trend that you didnt have to be rich to participate in.

Another trend was jean skirts. Jean skirts was a trend in the 80's as well and quickly became popular. Most people would make outfits from the off the shoulder tops and jean skirts. These skirts could range anywhere from $30-$100. This was another fairly cheap trend that a lot of people could participate in. Jean skirts came in the typical jean color and then widened to pink, blue, red, and yellow.
