Fashion has been a big part of society, especially New York, for thousands of years. Each year however new trends come into the world and old ones leave. Society is consistently buying new clothing pieces and accesories in order to keep up with the trends.

Along with the new year also comes new trends for the seasons. Each season has unique clothing outfits that people use to express themselves. Clothing stores have made a huge profit from clthing trends because they themsselves can sell them and have people buy them.
