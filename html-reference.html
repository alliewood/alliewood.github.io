Syntax

you use a <! - - at the begininng of the comment but then just add a - -> to end it. Your comment will go inbetween the two carrots.

You cant see the comment on the browser, just in the code.

Table

Description

A table is used to show a relationship between items and another variable. It can help you see relationships more clearly and it can be used to discover them as well.

Syntax

Each table needs <table> tag at the beginning and </table>at the end.Each row needs <tr> at the beginning and </tr> at the end. Each table header needs <th> at the beginning and </th> at the end. The table data needs <td> at the beginning and </td> at the end.

Example

<table> style="width:100%"> <tr> <th>Firstname</th> <th>Lastname</th> <th>grade</th> </tr> <tr> <td>alex </td> <td>bone </td> <td>5 </td> </tr> <tr> <td>dave </td> <td>Jackson </td> <td>10 </td> </tr> <tr> <td>sam </td> <td>smith </td> <td>12 </td> </tr> </table>

Display

Firstname Lastname grade
alex bone 5
dave Jackson 10
sam smith 12
